CinemaCam

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

CinemaCam ("the App") is a cinematic camera application for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Data We Do Not Collect

CinemaCam does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed
  • No location data is collected

Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera — required to capture photos and videos
  • Microphone — required to record audio during video capture
  • Photo Library — required to save captured photos and videos to your device

All captured media is saved directly to your device and never uploaded anywhere.

Third-Party Services

CinemaCam does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
